About Me

Hi, I'm Ming-Hsuan (Mingshen), a podcast creator and web developer with a background in Earth Science. After spending over six years studying the complexities of our planet, I decided to pivot my career towards storytelling and digital creation.

Through my podcast "Huh? Germany!", I explore cultural shocks and life reflections as I navigate living in Germany. My passion lies in sharing human stories and experiences that resonate with a diverse audience.

In addition to podcasting, I enjoy building websites and experimenting with web technologies to create engaging online experiences. Feel free to explore my projects and get in touch!
